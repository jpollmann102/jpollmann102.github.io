About Me

Graduated in 2019 from the University of Central Florida with a B.S. in Computer Science. Currently working at Walt Disney World in Orlando, FL.

Programming for the last 9 or so years, my strengths include JavaScript and Angular in web development, and Python scripting. My programming interests are in big data, UI design/implementation, and machine learning/AI.

For over three years I have been making magic with Disney, mainly doing full stack web development with Angular and Express/NodeJS. In college my senior design project had me focusing on big data organization and visualization for soundscape ecology researchers (check it out on my repos page).

More recently, I've been working a site over at ProTechSign for generating secure signature cards for business documents. This project has taught me so much about software product design and using Firebase and Google Cloud Platform which I am now a major fan of.

I'm also working on a site with Angular and Firebase for keeping track of gift ideas and your friends/loved ones' birthdays and anniversaries. Coming soon!

Outside of programming, I'm a huge basketball and football fan (including fantasy), I play a variety of video games on PC PlayStation and Switch, I'm really into board games, and I follow sumo wrestling.

Just taking things one day at a time, learning as I go to try and be the best programmer and person I can be.
